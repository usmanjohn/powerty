Our Logo, Our Mission

The Powerty logo embodies our core mission: transforming the challenge of GMAT/GRE/STAT preparation into an empowering journey. By crossing out "ty" in "poverty" and adding "v" to form "power", we symbolize the transition from limitation to strength through education.

Just as our name can be typed effortlessly on a single line of a QWERTY keyboard, we strive to make math preparation smooth, intuitive, and accessible for everyone.

Initial combination of P with G shows that GMAT can be a Power.
